Day 1 – Chest + Triceps (Push)

Focus: Upper chest, mid chest volume & stretch

  • Barbell Bench Press – 4x8
    Controlled form, pause momentarily on chest
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 4x10
    Bench inclined 30–45°, focus on upper chest
  • Chest Dips – 3x10–12
    Body slightly leaned forward to emphasize chest
    ✴️ Alternative: Machine Chest Press
  • Cable Crossover – 3x15
    D-Grip single handles, full stretch
  • Triceps Rope Pushdown – 3x12
    Rope handle, spread rope at end
  • Overhead Dumbbell Extension – 3x12
    With heavy dumbbell or EZ bar behind head
  • Cable Crunch – 3x15–20
    Focus on crunching abs, not pulling with hands

Day 2 – Back + Biceps (Pull)

Focus: Back width, thickness, biceps

  • Barbell Deadlift – 4x6
    Proper form, straight back, no jerking
  • Lat Pulldown (Wide Grip) – 4x10
    Wide grip, focus on stretch from back
  • One-Arm Dumbbell Row – 3x10
    Knee on bench, full contraction to side
  • Seated Cable Row (Close Grip V Handle) – 3x12
    Sit upright, avoid leaning back
  • Barbell Curl (Straight or EZ Bar) – 3x10
    Keep elbows fixed, controlled lowering
  • Hammer Curl (Dumbbell or Cable Rope) – 3x12
  • Leg Raises (Floor or Pull-up Bar) – 3x15

Day 3 – Legs

Focus: Quads, hamstrings, glutes

  • Barbell Back Squat – 4x8
    Bar on traps, depth below parallel
  • Leg Press (Wide Stance) – 4x10
    Targets quads and glutes
  • Romanian Deadlift – 3x10
    Hamstring stretch, keep back straight
  • Lying Leg Curl Machine – 3x12
    Controlled up and down motion
  • Walking Lunges (With Dumbbells) – 2x12/leg
    Deep steps, back straight
  • Standing Calf Raise (Machine or Barbell) – 4x15
    Pause at the top

Day 4 – Shoulders + Abs

Focus: Lateral & rear delts, upper traps

  • Overhead Dumbbell Press (Seated) – 4x8
    Controlled, no leaning back
  • Lateral Raise (Dumbbell) – 4x12
    Hands slightly forward, elbows slightly bent
  • Reverse Pec Deck (or Bent-Over Rear Delt Fly) – 3x15
    Focus on rear shoulder
  • Front Raise (Dumbbell or Plate) – 3x12
  • Dumbbell Shrugs – 3x15
  • Plank – 3x1 minute
  • Cable Woodchopper (D-Grip Single Hand) – 3x15/side

Day 5 – Isolation & Focus

Focus: Weak points and posture correction

  • Incline Cable Chest Fly – 3x12
  • Close-Grip Bench Press – 3x10
  • T-Bar Row – 3x8
  • Preacher Curl – 3x12
  • Cable Lateral Raise – 3x15
  • Face Pull – 3x15
  • Stretch + Foam Roll – 10–15 min
